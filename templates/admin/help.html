Defining the sidebar

The sidebar is specified in yaml. Here's an example sidebar definition: 
---
heading: ''

pages:
  - title: main
    id: 1

---
heading: A good topic

pages:
  - title: An article
    id: 2

  - title: Another article
    id: 3
{% endblock %}
